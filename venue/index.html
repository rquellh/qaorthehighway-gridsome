The Ohio Union

1739 N High St, Columbus, OH 43210

The Ohio Union is a 318,000 square foot facility built on a foundation of rich history and Buckeye spirit. Located on The Ohio State University’s campus, the building has 34 unique meeting and event spaces and is near many on-campus and off-campus dining and entertainment options.

Floor Plan
Food Options

During the conference, breakfast, lunch, and snacks will be provided.

However, if you want other options, there are several dining locations within the Ohio Union.
  • Union Market a fast-casual option offering grab-and-go food choices.
  • Espress-OH a coffee shop serving specialty drinks, breakfast items, and gelato.
Parking

Parking is free for attendees as long as you follow the following steps.

  1. Navigate to Ohio Union SOUTH Garage
    • 1759 N High St, Columbus, OH 43210
  2. Pull a ticket from the gate machine to enter the parking garage
  3. Bring that ticket with you to the conference registration desk
    • Someone at the registration desk will give you a barcoded validation sticker
  4. Exit the garage by inserting the entry ticket into the specified slot
